Our Story

A legacy of hospitality in Shenbaganur
Welcome to RS Green Valley, an exclusive retreat carefully woven into the pristine environment of Shenbaganur, Kodaikanal. We offer a haven for those seeking to escape the relentless pace of city life and immerse themselves deeply in the restorative tranquility of nature.

"Between every two pines is a doorway to a new world."

The Location

Perfectly Positioned.

Conveniently located opposite the Indian Petrol Bunk, our property is extraordinarily accessible while remaining secluded enough to provide a private, peaceful getaway.

From the misty, cool mornings watching the clouds roll over the deep green valleys to relaxing evenings gathered under a brilliant, unpolluted starlit sky, our goal is to provide an immersive experience that leaves you profoundly refreshed.

Every stone, every pathway, and every room has been designed to reflect the natural beauty of Kodaikanal, ensuring that whether you are indoors or out, you never feel disconnected from the environment.
